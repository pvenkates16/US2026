Jenny Lake Lodge DiningLodge fine dining

$$$$
Persephone BakeryBreakfast

$$
Old Faithful Inn DiningHistoric lodge

Bin22Wine bar & tapas

$$$
Grand Teton Scenic LoopFull day
Jenny Lake boat shuttle + Hidden Falls hike, Mormon Row Barns, Snake River Overlook.
Old Faithful & Upper Geyser BasinHalf day
Watch eruptions (~every 90 min), walk the boardwalks past dozens more geysers.
Grand Prismatic Spring2 hrs
Yellowstone's most photogenic spot. Hike Fairy Falls overlook — surreal rainbow colors from above.
Lamar Valley at DawnFull day
'Serengeti of North America.' Arrive at sunrise for wolves, grizzlies, bison herds. Bring binoculars.
Grand Canyon of YellowstoneHalf day
Artist Point and Uncle Tom's Trail for views of the thundering Lower Falls.
String Lake & Leigh LakeHalf day
Crystal alpine lakes at the Teton base. Perfect for kayaking, swimming, or a picnic.
Private Wildlife SafariFull day
Hire a guide (Teton Science Schools). They know where the animals are — worth every penny.
Start every day at 6-7 AM. Best wildlife sightings + no crowds.
Buy or rent bear spray from Jackson outdoor shops. Essential for all hikes.
No cell service in parks. Download offline maps before entering.
Gas stations are sparse inside parks — fill up whenever you see one.
With 6 people and 2 SUVs, split up: half hike while half do scenic drives.
Maneesh departs by Jul 22-25 to be back at work by Aug 3. Book his return flight from JAC early.
Book a sunrise hot-air balloon over the Tetons for a once-in-a-lifetime splurge.
